  production with a `TRADE_IN_PROVIDER=none` default — flipping it to

## Email 3 — RouteOne (Financing pre-qual, T1.6 primary)

**To:** `partners@routeone.com`
**Subject:** RouteOne partner integration — LotPilot adding financing pre-qual handoff for independent dealers

Hi RouteOne Partner Team,

I'm Mohammed Awad, founder of LotPilot ([https://lotpilot.com]). We
operate an AI sales assistant for independent used-car dealers and
we're ready to add a financing pre-qual handoff. RouteOne is our
primary choice for two reasons: your existing dealer footprint among
independent lots, and your soft-pull-first posture (which matches
ours — see our SSN policy below).

**Use case.** When a buyer in chat signals financing intent (asks
about monthly payment, asks if you "do bad credit," etc.), our AI
collects the minimum fields a soft-pull pre-qual needs (name + DOB +
address + `ssn_last4`), gates on TCPA-compliant consent, and POSTs
server-to-server to RouteOne. The buyer sees an acknowledgment +
estimated next-step copy; the dealer sees the pre-qual result in
their inbox with the buyer's Origination Lead linked.

**Posture your reviewer will want to confirm.**
- **SSN never transits our system in full form.** We accept
  `ssn_last4` only (4-char string, regex-validated). Our application
  layer hard-rejects any 9-digit SSN at the API boundary. The full
  SSN, if needed, is collected by the lender on the next-step page,
  not by us.
- Provider IDs are stored as `sha256(provider_id)` reference hashes —
  no raw FK to your system in our DB. We can dereference via a
  signed lookup if you need to audit a specific pre-qual.
- Server-to-server only. Dealer Origination ID per dealer, stored in
  our per-dealer settings table.
- Estimated **5-20 pre-quals per dealer per month** in pilot.
- Our scaffold (`src/lib/financing/route_one.ts`) is wired and waiting
  on the API contract — adapter swap is the only change once UAT
  passes.

**What we're asking for.**
1. The dealer Origination ID + soft-pull tier qualification process
   for a tech partner serving independent dealers.
2. UAT environment access + cert pathway timeline.
3. 30-min call with your integration team to confirm the adapter shape.

I'm happy to share our SSN-handling spec and TCPA consent flow ahead
of the call if that helps the technical reviewer.
